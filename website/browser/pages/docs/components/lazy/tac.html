Lazy

w-lazy defers rendering of its content until it scrolls into view, reserving placeholder space to avoid layout shift — matching Vuetify's VLazy. It activates via an IntersectionObserver (tune with root-margin / threshold) and reveals content with a transition (fade by default).

Deferred until visible

The wrapper holds min-height until the content enters the viewport, then swaps the shimmer placeholder for the slotted content.
