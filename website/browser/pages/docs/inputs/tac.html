Custom inputs

The w-field class and the w-input-group component are the foundation every DuVay form control is built on. They provide the label, hint, validation messages, prefix/suffix add-ons, and inline actions that wrap a raw w-input — use them directly to compose a control that isn’t covered by a dedicated component.

Field anatomy

A field is a label, a control, and an optional hint. Add w-field-error for the error state.
