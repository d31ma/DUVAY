Feedback

DuVay’s feedback components communicate loading, progress, and empty conditions. Each now has its own page:

  • Progress linear — horizontal progress bars with buffer, stream, and indeterminate states.
  • Progress circular — ring progress indicators, sized and colorable.
  • Skeleton loaders — shimmering placeholders composed from bone presets.
  • Empty states — illustrated “nothing here yet” surfaces with optional actions.
  • Alerts and Snackbars — inline and transient notifications.
