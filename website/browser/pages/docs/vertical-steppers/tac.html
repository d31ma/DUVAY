Vertical steppers

w-stepper-vertical stacks steps top-to-bottom and reveals each step's content inline as it becomes active — a good fit for forms on narrow screens. Each step is a w-stepper-vertical-item whose default slot is its panel content.

Basic

The active item expands its content; w-stepper-actions inside an item advances or rewinds the active step.
