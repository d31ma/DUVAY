Text and typography

DuVay ships a Material-3 type scale and a full set of text utility classes — typography, font weight, emphasis, alignment, decoration, transform, and truncation — mirroring the helpers documented on Vuetify's Text and typography page. Sizes, weights, and families are driven by design tokens, so the scale adapts with your theme.

Typography

Apply a role from the type scale with w-text-{role}-{size}. Roles are display, headline, title, body, and label; sizes are large, medium, and small.

Text alignment

Align text with text-left, text-center, text-right, and text-justify. Breakpoint variants w-text-md-* and w-text-lg-* apply from that width up.

Left aligned (.text-left)

Center aligned (.text-center)

Right aligned (.text-right)

Justified text stretches each line to both edges of its container so the left and right margins line up evenly down the block.

<p class="text-center w-text-md-left">Centered on mobile, left from md up</p>
